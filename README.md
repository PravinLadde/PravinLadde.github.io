*   **Role:** Lead Hardware Engineer (Concept to Production)
*   **Stack:** 6-Layer HDI, Blind/Buried Vias, Microvias, Battery Management (BMS).
*   **Challenge:** Integrating high-speed digital and sensitive analog circuits into a compact 30mm x 30mm form factor.
*   **Result:** Achieved 100% routing success; optimized power management for 7+ days of battery life.

*   **Result:** Passed industrial-grade EMI/EMC lab testing on the first spin; currently in mass production.

### 3. Custom Electromechanical Control Systems
*   **Stack:** Power Electronics, Rugged Mixed-Signal PCB Design, Fail-safe Architectures.
